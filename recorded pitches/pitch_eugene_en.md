# Pitch – Eugene (English)

I'm constantly fidgeting with something — with my rings, with my keys, with fidget toys. That's only a very base-level symptom of something deeper. Without ever being diagnosed, I'm probably somewhere on the ADHD/ADD spectrum. Not sure where. And there are a lot of people like that.

It's getting worse because of media addiction, shortened attention spans, and so on. It's a growing thing that people kind of struggle with — and we want to help them.

We want to help them by giving them an accountability partner. Somebody who reminds them of what they have to do, what they have to achieve, and what they want to achieve — and helps them get there.

How do we do that? Pretty easy. We have a task bot — an accountability bot — that helps you get through a week, helps you achieve your tasks by actually reminding you in the way you want or need to be reminded. It helps you. It learns to know you. It learns to understand you, and helps you in the way that you actually need it.

You need more reward? Great! You need more of a punishment for when you don't do it? Great! You need constant calls that actually remind you that you have to do things? It can do that as well.

That's what we are building.
