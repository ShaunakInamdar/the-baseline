# Pitch – Shaunak (English)

What are we building? We're building a goal achiever. What I mean by that is Only Plans — the name of our app. It's a task board that's going to hold you accountable to your tasks, and it's meant for you as a person.

It's not only going to track your tasks, deadlines, and goals that you want to achieve. It's going to first understand you as a person — understand your needs through a subjective interview. It could be an interview; it could be a puzzle game — something to extract the way your mind works.

It's going to ask you for what you want to achieve, and it's going to hold your hand till the end. It's going to make sure it manages your deadlines, schedules your tasks, and breaks your major tasks into subtasks. It's going to follow up, remind you, follow through, and give you rewards when you complete tasks — based on the size of the task itself.

It's going to help you achieve your goals at the end of your own imposed deadline.
