# Pitch – Oliver (English)

Hey everyone, this is our pitch and my name is Oliver. We're building an app that helps people with ADHD — and honestly everyone else too — achieve their goals.

You probably know the feeling: you've got a hundred thousand projects, thirty different tasks you can't organize, no idea what's most important or when to do what. And when you fall into a rabbit hole, you completely lose the overview — you end up doing something overly complicated that takes way longer than expected, and lose sight of the big picture entirely.

That's why we built Only Plans. It helps you break your tasks into small, actionable steps, acts as an accountability partner that calls you multiple times a day, keeps you on track, and helps you meet your deadlines and set your priorities correctly.

Our call reminder also makes sure that when you're working alone, you don't drift off and end up doing something totally unimportant — instead staying focused on what actually matters. With a simple, very clear overview, the tool helps you achieve all your life goals.
