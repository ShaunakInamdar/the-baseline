# Pitch – Ramona (English)

Hi, I'm Ramona, and I want to ask you something: do you know this feeling? End of the week comes around and you think — "Wow, where did the time go? I had so much to do but I got absolutely nothing done."

That's exactly why — and because this is something many of you probably relate to, and especially because it's a massive problem for people with ADHD — we built Only Plans. It's an app designed to help you structure your day, and it connects directly to your calendar.

An agent will remind you by calling you and repeatedly bringing your focus back to whatever matters most to you. At the same time, it makes sure all the side tasks — things like household chores, doing your taxes, or preparing for a birthday — don't slip through the cracks.

All of this helps you figure out what your actual priorities in life are, and which tasks matter most to you. It will remind you when an important deadline is approaching.

On top of that, you earn small benefits and rewards that keep you engaged and motivated — because people with ADHD in particular can get scattered by their emotions. They can hyperfocus brilliantly, but distractions can easily throw them off course.

That's exactly why we built Only Plans — to make life easier for you and everyone dealing with ADHD.
