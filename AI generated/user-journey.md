# Only Plans — User Journey

## 1. Signup

The user lands on the Only Plans website and signs up with:
- **Name**
- **Email address**
- **Phone number** *(required — this is the channel the AI agent uses to reach them)*

No password needed initially. Phone number is verified via SMS code.

---

## 2. Onboarding

A conversational onboarding — delivered via **voice call + text** — that builds the user's personal profile ("mindprint"). The AI asks:

**Time & Schedule**
- "What time do you usually start your day?"
- "Are there time windows where you're off-limits? (gym, kids, commute)"

**Goals & First Task**
- "What's the one thing you most want to get done this week?"
- "Is it a one-time task or part of a bigger goal?"
- *→ AI breaks it down into subtasks and creates the first Task Card on the board*

**Motivation Style**
- "How do you want to be treated when you're off-track — tough love, gentle nudge, or just the facts?"
- "Does a reward motivate you more, or does the fear of missing a deadline?"

**Priority & Urgency**
- "How important is this to you on a scale of 1–10?"
- "Is there a hard deadline, or is this open-ended?"

**Call Preferences**
- "How often do you want me to check in — every hour, a few times a day, or just once?"
- "What should I do if you don't pick up?"

**Onboarding Confirmation (Feedback to User)**
> The AI reads the task back to the user:
> *"Okay — your top priority is [Task]. I'll check in at [Time]. If you miss my call, I'll send a text. Sound right?"*

The Task Card is created on the board with: title, subtasks, priority level, deadline, and call schedule.

---

## 3. The Accountability Call (Core Loop)

At the scheduled time, **Only Plans calls the user.**

**Opening:**
> *"Hey, it's Only Plans. You set [Task] as your priority today. Let's check in — how's it going?"*

**Status Update (user picks one):**
- **Not started** → "What's getting in the way? Want me to call you back in 30 minutes to get you going?"
- **In progress** → "Nice — keep going. I'll check in again at [Time]."
- **Can't finish** → "No problem. Want to reschedule this, or break it into something smaller?"

**Follow-up offer:**
> *"Do you want a second call later today to wrap this up?"*

After the call, the AI automatically updates the Task Card status and adjusts the schedule if needed.

---

## 4. Task Board & Overview

After each call, the user can see their live board:
- Task Cards with current status (Not Started / In Progress / Done)
- Today's priority, highlighted
- Upcoming calls and reminders
- Progress toward the bigger goal

---

## 5. Completion & Reward

When the user marks a task as done (or confirms it on a call):
- The Task Card moves to **Done**
- The user receives a **reward** scaled to the task's difficulty/priority
- The AI acknowledges the win:
  > *"You did it. [Task] is done. That was a [Hard/Medium/Easy] one — you earned [Reward]."*
