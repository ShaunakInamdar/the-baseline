# Only Plans – Landing Page Copy

---

## 🇬🇧 English Version

### Headline
**Finally get things done. Only Plans.**

### Subheadline
Your AI accountability partner — built for the way your brain actually works.

### Problem Statement
You know that feeling. End of the week, you look back and think: *"Where did the time go? I had so much to do — and got nothing done."*

You had the intention. You had the energy. But somewhere between the hundred open tabs, the thirty tasks, and the rabbit hole you didn't plan to fall into — the important things just didn't happen.

Sound familiar? You're not alone. Whether you've been diagnosed with ADHD, suspect you might be somewhere on the spectrum, or simply struggle with focus in a world designed to distract you — Only Plans was built for you.

### Solution
**Only Plans** is your personal goal achievement system. It doesn't just track your tasks — it understands you first.

Through a personalized onboarding (an interview, a game, or a reflection), Only Plans learns how your mind works, what motivates you, and what you want to achieve. Then it builds a structure around *your* life — not a generic template.

### Key Features
- 📞 **AI accountability calls** — your agent checks in on you multiple times a day, brings you back to focus, and keeps you on track
- ✅ **Smart task breakdown** — big goals get split into small, doable steps you can actually tackle
- 📅 **Calendar integration** — your tasks live alongside your real schedule
- 🏆 **Reward system** — earn benefits as you complete tasks, scaled to how hard the task was
- 🔔 **Deadline reminders** — never miss what matters, including the small stuff like birthdays and taxes
- 🗂️ **Clear overview** — always know what your most important task is, right now.

### Who It's For
Only Plans is for anyone who wants to stop losing time and start achieving their goals:
- People with ADHD or ADD (diagnosed or not)
- Anyone overwhelmed by too many tasks and no clear priorities
- Solo workers who need an external anchor to stay on track
- People whose attention span is suffering from the modern world — and know it

### Closing Statement
Stop planning to plan. Only Plans gives you a system that holds your hand, calls you out, and celebrates every win — until your goals are done.

**Your goals. Your deadline. Your accountability partner.**
*Only Plans.*
