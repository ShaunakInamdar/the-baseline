# Only Plans — Onboarding Script (Base Layer)

> Universal onboarding for all users. No assumptions about diagnosis or work style.
> The ADHD-specific script layers on top of this.

---

```
AGENT: Hey, welcome to Only Plans. I'm your accountability partner.
       Quick intro before we dive in — I'm going to ask you a few
       questions so I can actually be useful to you.
       Takes about 3 minutes. Let's go.

--- SECTION 1: TIME & AVAILABILITY ---

AGENT: When during the day do you do your best work?

       [morning / afternoon / evening / varies]

AGENT: Are there times I should never contact you?
       Think: meetings, gym, family time, sleep.

       [user responds]

--- SECTION 2: YOUR GOAL ---

AGENT: What's the one thing you most want to get done?

       [user responds]

AGENT: Why does that matter to you right now?

       [user responds]

AGENT: What's the first concrete step you could take on that?

       [user responds — AI creates first task card]

--- SECTION 3: HOW YOU WORK ---

AGENT: Do you prefer a clear structure for your day,
       or do you like to keep things flexible?

       [structured / flexible / mix]

AGENT: How do you usually like to be held accountable?

       [check-ins keep me on track
        I prefer to self-report
        I just need reminders
        I'm not sure yet]

--- SECTION 4: COMMUNICATION STYLE ---

AGENT: How should I talk to you when things aren't going to plan?

       [be direct — just tell me
        be encouraging — I need motivation
        keep it neutral — no pressure]

AGENT: How often do you want me to check in?

       [a few times a day / once a day / a few times a week]

AGENT: If you don't pick up when I call — what should I do?

       [try again in 30 min / send a text / wait for me to reach out]

--- SECTION 5: CONFIRM & CREATE ---

AGENT: Here's what I have:

       Your first task is: [TASK]
       First step: [STEP]
       I'll check in at [TIME].
       If you don't pick up, I'll [FALLBACK].
       I'll keep things [TONE].

       Does that sound right?

       [yes / change something]

AGENT: Great. Your task card is ready.
       I'll talk to you at [TIME].
```
