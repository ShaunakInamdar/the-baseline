How It Works

Three steps to focus.

1

Only Plans gets to know you

During onboarding you answer a few questions — or play a quick game. This helps the AI understand how your mind works and what truly motivates you.

2

Your goals get structured

You enter your tasks. Only Plans breaks them into small steps, prioritizes them, and places them in your calendar — realistically and actionably.

3

Your agent keeps you on track

Multiple times a day your AI agent calls you. It brings you back on course, celebrates your wins — and adapts to how much accountability you need.
